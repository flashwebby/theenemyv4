Character Sketches

Dr. Sadao Hoki

A skilled surgeon torn between his duty as a doctor and loyalty to his nation, representing the core conflict.

Hana

Sadao’s compassionate wife who supports his humanitarian choice despite her own fear and societal pressure.

Tom

A young, wounded American POW who represents the "enemy" but becomes a symbol of shared humanity.

The General

A self-absorbed, pragmatic leader who embodies the impersonal and often negligent nature of authority.
